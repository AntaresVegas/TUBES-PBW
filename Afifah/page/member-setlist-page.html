About Us

Welcome to CapiList, the best place to discover setlists of your favorite artists at various music festivals! CapiList makes it easy for you to get updated setlist information from festivals worldwide. Thank you for visiting, and we hope you're inspired by the amazing artist setlists on CapiList!
